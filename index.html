A hello from Fasal!

Fasal is an agri-tech company reshaping horticulture and the agri value chain. On the farm side, our technology continues touching the lives of over 5,000 Indian farmers with precision horticulture. They continue reaping the benefits of pest and disease-free yields with increased profits firsthand.

Beyond farms, we are reviving a rigid agri value chain and shorter delivery cycles to ensure farm-fresh quality for consumers consistently. The produce we source comes only from farms with a Fasal system with minimal handling allowing us to ensure quality consistency with an optimised delivery cycle.
